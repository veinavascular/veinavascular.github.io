Our Team

At Veina, we are committed to transforming the way we address hemolysis in the emergency department. Our journey started with a deep understanding of the challenges faced by healthcare professionals in managing blood samples, particularly the high rates of hemolysis that impact patient care and efficiency. Driven by this insight, we have been dedicated to developing a groundbreaking solution that reduces hemolysis and improves patient outcomes. Our team is working relentlessly to introduce an innovative, non-invasive technology that will not only enhance blood sample integrity but also streamline workflows in the ED. Connect with us to learn more about how our cutting-edge solutions can help reduce hemolysis and optimize care delivery.
